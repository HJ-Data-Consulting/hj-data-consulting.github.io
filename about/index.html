Engineering Standards

HJ Consulting provides elite cloud architecture for organizations that require absolute reliability. We don't build demos; we build systems designed with judgment, restraint, and technical precision.

As a founder in the **Google for Startups Cloud Program**, my practice operates at a level compatible with Google’s internal engineering standards. Every project we undertake is a commitment to scalable, defensible architecture.

We prioritize sound abstractions, principled tradeoffs, and systems designed for real-world failure cases. If a decision cannot be defended under rigorous design review, it does not belong in our systems.

Elite Systems Engineering

Elite engineering is about more than just scale—it's about choosing the simplest architecture that survives reality. We specialize in understanding complex tradeoffs and designing for failure, cost, and the humans who operate them.

Defensible Architecture

We treat engineering decisions as the primary product. Transparency and credibility are our core metrics. Every system we build is designed to meet the standards expected by Staff Engineers and SREs at organizations like Google.
