About Herman Justino & HJ Data Consulting

I'm Herman Justino, a Master's student in Data Science and founder of HJ Data Consulting, an Ontario-based company specializing in data-centric, interactive web tools. I combine academic training and entrepreneurial experience to build useful products that solve real-world problems.

DaRMoD Program & Vector Institute

I've recently been accepted into the DaRMoD (Data Readiness, Model Development & Model Deployment) cohort through the Vector Institute—an intensive program aimed at helping Canadian SMEs build real-world AI solutions with expert support. This participation provides access to lectures, mentorship, and a recognized certificate from the Vector Institute, strengthening both the technical foundation and credibility of our projects.

Current Project: Clinical Note Summarization

During the DaRMoD program, I am developing a health tech solution for Ontario healthcare providers. The core focus is clinical note summarization—building AI-powered tools that can take unstructured clinical documentation and generate concise, usable summaries. The goal is to surface essential information quickly, reducing clinician burden and improving decision-making efficiency.

This project has significant commercial potential as a productized tool for healthcare providers and institutions, addressing a critical need in the healthcare system while leveraging cutting-edge AI capabilities.

Why This Matters
